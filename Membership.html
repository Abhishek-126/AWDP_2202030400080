Membership Plans

Join Sport Academy and become a member to enjoy exclusive benefits and access to our top-notch facilities. Choose a membership plan that suits your needs and kickstart your journey to a healthier and more active lifestyle.

Membership Benefits:

  • Access to all sports facilities
  • Personalized training sessions
  • Discounts on special events
  • Free participation in member-only tournaments

Membership Plans:

  • Basic Membership: Access to one sport facility - $50/month
  • Silver Membership: Access to two sport facilities - $75/month
  • Gold Membership: Access to all sport facilities - $100/month
